Movie Industry Is Not Recovering Following Reopening

By Richard Heaton

October 11th, 2020

The movie industry is in turmoil following post-Covid-19 theater openings.

In 2019, movies averaged a domestic gross of more than $100 million per week but 2020 has been a different story. After being shut down for six months due to the virus, many theaters remained closed. Those that did, saw huge drops in attendance as movie-goers became hesitant to watch movies with others.

As a result, the domestic box office gross took a hit of 80-95% during the last two months.
